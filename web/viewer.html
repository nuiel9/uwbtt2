About

This is the viewer for a UWB (ultra-wideband) indoor positioning system. A tag ranges to three fixed anchors; the distances go to a database, and this page turns them into a live position on the map.

Setup — calibration, geometry and the trigger areas — is done on the separate control site. This viewer is read-only.

Areas and options are read live from the control site, so anything you change there shows up here.
